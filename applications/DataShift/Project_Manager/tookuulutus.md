## Position: Project Manager

### What You'll Do

- **Manage the full lifecycle** of web projects, from planning to delivery.
- **Coordinate across teams** of developers, designers, QA, and stakeholders.
- **Create and manage tasks** in Jira, ensuring smooth workflows and transparent progress tracking.
- **Conduct A/B tests,** analyze results, and translate insights into actionable improvements for user experience.
- **Optimize website** usability and functionality.
- **Write compelling,** clear, and creative marketing content.

### What's Important to Us

- You're **organized** and see projects through from start to finish.
- **Strong English and Russian** communication skills.
- **Copywriting skills** and a good eye for engaging content.
- **Familiarity with content creation processes** (text, design, layout) and Jira (or similar tools).
- **Proactive, highly organized,** and able to handle multiple projects simultaneously.

### What We Offer

- **Competitive salary,** performance bonuses, and annual pay raises.
- **Sports compensation** and wellness perks via **Stebby,** plus **health insurance.**
- **Exciting events** like the Annual Gala, summer parties, and team-building activities.
- **Participation in large-scale conferences** with travel opportunities worldwide.
- **Celebrations** of milestones and special moments, such as birthdays, work anniversaries, and other achievements.
- Work in a **modern office** located in the city center **with free parking.**
