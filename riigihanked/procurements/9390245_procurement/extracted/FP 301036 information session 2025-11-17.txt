Why
•

ESTDEV is increasingly implementing digitalisation and cyber
security projects around the world

•

Growing number of international donors and partners

•

Since September 2025 ESTDEV has European Commission
accreditation for managing and implementing EU funds

•

As an example, ESTDEV implements the CURE cybersecurity
project under the Tallinn Mechanism
•

Tallinn Mechanism is a coordination mechanism for civilian sector
cybersecurity support to Ukraine established in December 2023

•

Aims to strengthen Ukraine’s long-term systematic preparedness and
resilience against cyber threats.

Example project - CURE
Recipients

Secretariat of the Cabinet of

Ministry of Veterans Affairs

Outputs
•

Secure and resilient cybersecurity infrastructure established for selected critical Ukrainian government institutions

•

Improved institutional cybersecurity governance and capacity to manage threats in selected Ukrainian government

•

Increased compliance with international cybersecurity standards

What
On Nov 13th ESTDEV published a framework procurement which over the next 4 years will enable us to
deliver:
•

Lot 1: Information and cybersecurity consulting services

•

Lot 2: Information and cybersecurity software analysis, development and applications

•

Lot 3: Various IT and cybersecurity infrastructure components, installation and configuration

Team requirements by skills & experience
• 60 months of work experience as a project manager/consultant/expert/architect/sysadmin in
the field of information and cyber security.
• Experience in the given role in at least 10 information and cyber security projects, of which at
least 5 must be international.
• Projects must have been completed within the last 5 years.
• At least 5 projects must have a minimum financial volume of 50000 euros (excluding VAT).

• In at least 5 projects, the consultant's time contribution must be at least 100 hours.
• Project manager’s language proficiency in English must be at least at the C1 level.
• More specific requirements may be included in individual minitenders.
