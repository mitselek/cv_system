**What is CISA?**

- International professional certification for IS auditors
- Issued by ISACA (Information Systems Audit and Control Association)
- Requires passing 4-hour, 150-question exam
- Requires 5 years of professional IS audit/security/control work experience
- Annual renewal: 20+ CPE hours + $85 maintenance fee
- Exam cost: $575 (members) or $760 (non-members)
- Study time: 3-6 months typical preparation

**Can You Obtain It Quickly?**

NO - Minimum timeline:

- Join ISACA: 1 day
- Study for exam: 3-6 months minimum
- Schedule exam: 2-4 weeks wait
- Pass exam: 1 day
- Total: **4-7 months minimum**

**Deadline:** 12 days (2025-12-09) - **IMPOSSIBLE to obtain in time**

**Gap Severity:** CRITICAL - This is a hard, non-negotiable requirement.
