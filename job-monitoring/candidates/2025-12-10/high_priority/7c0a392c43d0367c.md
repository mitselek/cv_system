## Job Description

Join our client's innovative team and help shape the future of cryostat systems.

Bluefors is a leader in cryogenics, designing groundbreaking measurement devices for their advanced cryostat systems.

As a Senior Embedded Developer, you get to work in a collaborative, innovative and technically challenging environment.

If this sounds an excellent next step for you, apply today! About the role As a Senior Embedded Developer, you will be a part of Bluefors' Temperature Controller team and focus on refactoring and improving a long-standing embedded software codebase, fixing bugs, closing gaps, and ensuring maintainability of a measurement device.

You will also participate in designing and implementing new features and participate in testing activities.

Collaboration with engineers, physicists, and technicians is essential to understand device behaviour and support system integration.

To be successful in this role, you have an excellent understanding working with embedded softwares and ability to cooperate and communicate with different stakeholders.

Technologies and tools Linux Yocto Python Git You are offered A senior role in a technically challenging, innovative and global environment Possibility to work with interesting work tasks and with a collaborative and supportive team Flexible hybrid work model, combining weekly office presence and remote work Work tasks Refactoring and improving existing embedded software for a critical measurement device Fixing bugs, closing functional gaps, and ensuring a code maintainability Participating in designing and implementing of a new features and functionalities for the measurement device Collaborating with various stakeholders Contributing to architectural discussions and strategic planning for future product evolution We are looking for Degree in a relevant field of engineering Strong hands-on experience in embedded software development Advanced knowledge of Linux, Yocto, Python, and Git Experience working with software architecture Excellent collaboration and interaction skills and ability to work with different stakeholders Proactive and independent way of working Fluency in English It is meritorious if you have Knowledge of Angular Experience working with test automation Ability to leverage AI tools for code analysis and optimization Experience working in the manufacturing industry Experience working with measurement devices.
