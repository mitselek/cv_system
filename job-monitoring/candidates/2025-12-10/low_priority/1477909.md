## Job Description

Are you passionate about electrical design and eager to work on large-scale international projects? We are looking for a skilled secondary systems designer to join our team, specializing in medium and high voltage substations, control circuit, and auxiliary design.

The project are mostly located in Finland and Baltics, but sometimes in other countries.

Your Profile: Degree in Electrical Engineering or Electrical Technician qualification.

Minimum of 3 years of hands-on experience in electrical design and engineering.

Proficiency in design software (e.g., Eplan) and Microsoft Office.

Fluency in written and spoken English; (Finnish or Estonian language skills are an asset).

Ability to work independently as well as collaboratively within a team.

Strong ownership and the attention to details and accuracy.

Good communication skills and cultural awareness.

Willingness to travel occasionally and visit project sites.

Valid driving license.

Key Responsibilities:: Design and engineer relay protection, control circuits, and auxiliary systems for medium and high voltage substations.

Prepare and review technical documentation, drawings, and schematics.

Collaborate with project teams to ensure designs meet project requirements and timelines.

Communicate effectively with customers, colleagues, and stakeholders from diverse backgrounds.

Ensure compliance with industry standards and safety regulations.

What We Offer:: Competitive salary with additional performance-based bonuses.

Flexible working arrangements (office/home).

Additional paid vocation days Additional health insurance Sport compensation Opportunity to work on large-scale international projects.

Long-term career development and the chance to become an expert in your field.

Support for expanding your technical knowledge, including training opportunities.

Supportive colleagues with good team spirit.
