## Job Description

The Impact You'll Make At Danfoss, we're shaping the future of industrial automation-and you can be part of that journey.

As a Product Marketing Manager for Industrial Drives, you'll play a key role in accelerating growth and helping customers unlock new possibilities in their applications.

This is your chance to influence how cutting-edge drive technology reaches the market.

You'll work closely with system integrators and strategic partners, ensuring they have the tools, insights, and support to succeed.

Your work will directly impact adoption of our flagship product line, which is set to transform the industry by 2030.

Join a global team that thrives on collaboration and innovation.

If you're passionate about technology, value creation, and building strong relationships, this role offers a unique opportunity to make a lasting difference.

You will be working out of Helsinki, Vaasa or Tampere.

What You'll Be Doing Drive growth by shaping product strategies and value propositions for iC7-Automation high-power drives Build and strengthen partnerships with system integrators through programs like our Integrator Expert Program, creating tools and resources that deliver real value Collaborate globally with sales teams, product managers, and vertical experts to identify market trends and turn insights into action Champion customer needs by engaging with key accounts and translating their requirements into product features and solutions Support innovation by contributing to product development decisions and ensuring our offerings meet evolving market demands What We're Looking For A technical degree (B.Sc.

Danfoss engineers solutions that increase machine productivity, reduce emissions, lower energy consumption, and enable electrification.

Our solutions are used in such areas as refrigeration, air conditioning, heating, power conversion, motor control, industrial machinery, automotive, marine, and off- and on-highway equipment.

We also provide solutions for renewable energy, such as solar and wind power, as well as district-energy infrastructure for cities.

Our innovative engineering dates back to 1933.

Danfoss is family-owned, employing more than 39.000 people, serving customers in more than 100 countries through a global footprint of 95 factories.
