## Job Description

Do you have a keen interest in financial markets & financial instruments? Do you want to explore & communicate the impacts of Financial Risks to a bank? In this position you will have opportunity to: Build a solid understanding among stakeholders about Financial Risk profile of a bank (Market, Liquidity, Capital + Counterparty Credit Risk); Prepare a regular analysis and reports on the bank's Financial Risk profile in Estonia & Baltics, communicate results to stakeholders & top management; Continuously contribute to improvements of Financial Risk management processes, models, governance in Estonia & Baltics; Grow and develop your competence in Financial Risk management area.

What is needed in this role: Academic degree in relevant area; To have a positive attitude & be strong team-player; Ability to work independently, while actively collaborating with diverse cross-border teams with Risk, Treasury & Markets units; Good knowledge of financial markets products, risks & relevant regulations (CRR, EBA, etc); Fluent in written and spoken English and Estonian; Advanced skills in MS Excel; Experience from VBA/SQL/R/Python/Power BI or other similar software is meriting.

With us, you can experience: Personal and professional growth through self-leadership and continuous development; Meaningful work that positively impacts our workplace, our customers, and society; An open and collaborative culture that encourages cross-functional teamwork and provides networking opportunities; A supportive and inclusive environment that promotes a balanced and sustainable work-life, with flexible working conditions when suitable for the role; Benefits such as our share based reward program Eken, company pension plan, employee offer for banking products, health insurance.
