## Job Description

ISE is seeking to appoint a part-time Estonian Language & Culture Teacher to start in February 2026, with the strong possibility of the role growing into a full-time position.

International School of Estonia is a private, co-educational, not-for-profit school, located in Estonia (Tallinn).

ISE is a fully authorised International Baccalaureate Continuum World School.

It is also accredited with NEASC and CIS, and as such, is the only triple authorised/ accredited International School in Estonia.

Qualifications /prerequisites Degree-qualified, certified Teacher with a formal, degree-level studies in language education, preferably with a focus on Estonian.

At least 2 years of experience in a similar position.

Additional Desirable Qualifications: Commitment to model the behavioural competencies of the IB Learner Profile Training and experience teaching the IB Primary Years Programme of the International Baccalaureate An understanding of inquiry learning and the importance of student agency.

Good skills using instructional technology and experience in differentiated classroom teaching.

A knowledge of/passion for the International Baccalaureate, Strong desire to be an inspiring, motivated educator.

Practical English language-based experience in a similar position.

The Role This part-time position involves teaching students in the primary and middle school.

Working Relationships You will form part of a small, closely knit cohort of primary teachers in the school who work collaboratively to maximise learning opportunities for students.

The Package In addition to a competitive salary, an accommodation allowance, relocation support, and high-quality international medical insurance is available to overseas candidates.

Expressions of interest for the above position close on 31 January 2026.

Should a very suitable applicant be identified before that date, the school reserves the right to make an immediate appointment.

A letter of interest and introduction, a Curriculum Vitae and the names and contact details of two referees who have been closely involved with you professionally should be sent by email to: vacancies@ise.edu.ee Please note: The school reserves the right to appoint a suitable candidate prior to the closing date.

Only candidates shortlisted for an interview will be contacted.

Further information about ISE may be found at https://ise.edu.ee Katre Pohlak HR Manager
