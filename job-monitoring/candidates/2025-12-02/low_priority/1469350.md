## Job Description

Join the Future of European Connectivity Are you ready to lead complex infrastructure works that shape the future of transport across Europe? We're looking for a skilled Earthworks Site Project Manager to join Budimex and the Rail Baltic project in Estonia - a transformative development connecting the Baltic States with the rest of Europe.

This is a unique opportunity to work on a high-impact project delivered in an Alliance model, where collaboration, transparency, and shared success are key.

Your Mission as a Site Project Manager, you will be responsible for: Leading the design and coordination of technical solutions with designers, clients, and internal teams.

Ensuring project delivery on time, within budget, and in compliance with HSE standards.

Minimizing environmental and social impact, especially near protected Natura 2000 areas.

Managing subcontractors and service providers, ensuring quality and performance.

Overseeing documentation and reporting using Common Data Environment (CDE) platforms.

Taking ownership of budgeting and cost control within assigned work packages.

What You Bring: Education: Degree in Civil Engineering.

Certification: Valid authorization to manage large-scale construction works.

Experience: Proven track record in large-scale earthworks and infrastructure projects.

Technical Skills: Proficiency in CAD, MS Office, and familiarity with CDE platforms.

Experience with Last Planner System and Situational Awareness Systems is a plus.

Language: Good knowledge of English verbally and written.

Soft Skills That Set You Apart: Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Ability to thrive in multicultural, international teams.

Strong sense of collaboration, transparency, and respect for cultural differences.

High resilience and adaptability in fast-paced environments.

Why Join Budimex? Be part of a landmark European infrastructure project.

Work in a collaborative Alliance model with leading industry partners.

Make a real impact on sustainable development and regional connectivity.

Grow your career in a dynamic, international environment.
