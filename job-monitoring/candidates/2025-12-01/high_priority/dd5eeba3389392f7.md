Why Verifone For more than 30 years Verifone has established a remarkable record of leadership in the electronic payment technology industry.

Verifone has one of the leading electronic payment solutions brands and is one of the largest providers of electronic payment systems worldwide.

Verifone has a diverse, dynamic and fast paced work environment in which employees are focused on results and have opportunities to excel.

We take pride in the fact that we work with leading retailers, merchants, banks, and third-party partners to invent and deliver innovative payments solution around the world.

We strive for excellence in our products and services, and are obsessed with customer happiness.

Across the globe, Verifone employees are leading the payments industry through experience, innovation, and an ambitious spirit.

Whether it's developing the next platform of secure payment systems or searching for new ways to bring electronic payments to new markets, the team at Verifone is dedicated to the success of our customers, partners and investors.

It is this passion for innovation that drives each one of our employees for personal and professional success.

What's Exciting About the Role Verifone is looking to hire an exceptional Lead Software Development Engineer/Architect to take our payment services in the Nordics & Baltics region to the next level! You will lead the design and the hands-on implementation work related to new functionalities and optimization of our solutions using Agile methodologies.

The Lead Software Development Engineer/Architect will work in an exciting international environment and collaborate with other Verifone associates in multi-functional teams.

You will be a part of global initiatives and have the opportunity to build a career in one of the leading companies in the payments industry worldwide! Skills and Experience We Desire Bachelor's or Master's Degree in Computer Science or Engineering, or similar years of professional and relevant experience.

5+ years of experience with Java or other object-oriented languages, especially on backend solutions.

Wide knowledge of different tech stacks and supporting technologies available around Java-based systems.

Experience in running services on Linux.

Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.

Proactive attitude with good communication and collaboration skills.

Knowledge of Scrum and other Agile methodologies.

Hands-on experience with SQL databases.

Familiarity with basic network security concepts and knowledge of cryptography.

Fluent written and spoken English.

Preferred Skills Payments industry and solutions knowledge.

Experience leading a team, working as a systems architect, and/or project management skills.

Front-end development experience (e.g.

JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3) and knowledge of frameworks/technologies.
