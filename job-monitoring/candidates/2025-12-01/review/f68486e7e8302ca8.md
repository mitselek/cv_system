## Job Description

The Impact You'll Make At Danfoss Drives, we're driving the transformation toward a smarter, safer, and more energy-efficient future.

As our new Technical Product Manager, Cybersecurity, you'll play a key role in protecting the technology that powers sustainable industry - ensuring our electrical motor drives meet the highest global cybersecurity standards You'll help shape how cybersecurity comes to life in our products - from design to certification - giving customers the confidence that their automation systems are secure and future-ready.

This is your opportunity to make a tangible impact at the intersection of innovation, compliance, and safety Join a team that values collaboration, continuous learning, and doing work that truly matters.

Together, we'll set new benchmarks for cybersecurity in industrial automation Position is located: Vaasa, Tampere or Helsinki What You'll Be Doing Lead and manage cybersecurity certification activities (IEC62443-4) for our drive products, working closely with skilled development and compliance teams.

Collaborate with internal R&D, product management, and customers to align on cybersecurity goals and deliver trusted, compliant solutions.

Guide documentation and validation processes that demonstrate compliance throughout the product lifecycle.

Supervise and maintain security lifecycle activities, ensuring consistent, evidence-based certification outcomes.

Support continuous improvement by sharing best practices and identifying opportunities to enhance security processes.

What We're Looking For Proven experience in embedded device cybersecurity development and certification.

Hands-on knowledge of the IEC62443-4 cybersecurity certification process - including document management, audits, and implementation.

Degree (B.Sc.

or M.Sc.) in computer science, automation, or a related engineering field.

Structured, self-driven mindset with strong communication and stakeholder management skills.

Experience in functional safety (IEC61508) or electrical motor drives is an advantage.

Danfoss engineers solutions that increase machine productivity, reduce emissions, lower energy consumption, and enable electrification.
