## Job Description

We're on a mission to make data work easier, faster, and a lot more fun - with one clear goal: creating measurable business impact.

Agile Data Engine (ADE) helps organizations automate the heavy lifting of building and maintaining modern cloud-based data platforms.

Our team bridges product development and hands-on customer work - and that's where you come in.

We're looking for a Solution Architect who loves designing elegant, scalable solutions and guiding customers on how to get the most out of their data.

You'll be the key link between our customers, product team, and the ADE community - helping shape architectures, onboard new users, and ensure projects turn into long-term success stories.

What you'll do Design and guide customer architectures that are scalable, secure, and future-proof Lead technical onboarding and ensure smooth project kick-offs Act as a trusted advisor - turning complex business needs into smart, data-driven solutions Be part of leading the change toward AI-driven data work - helping customers understand and harness the power of AI and automation in their data platforms Be hands-on with modern cloud technologies such as Snowflake, Databricks,Google Cloud, Microsoft Fabric and Agile Data Engine (ADE) Build and share reusable assets like documentation, GitHub examples, and training materials Collaborate closely with ADE's product and engineering teams to influence the roadmap with real-world insights Solve challenging customer cases (3rd-level support) when things get tricky Share your knowledge through community talks, articles, or events What makes you a great fit Good understanding of Agile Data Engine (ADE) or motivation to deepen your expertise with it while working alongside our product and customer teams 3+ years of experience in solution architecture, data architecture, or system design , ideally in SaaS or B2B environments Strong understanding of cloud platforms, data modeling, and integration patterns Proven ability to translate business goals into scalable, maintainable technical designs Curiosity for AI, automation, and emerging data technologies Bachelor's degree in Computer Science , Engineering, or a relevant field is preferred A proactive, collaborative mindset - you make things happen and help others succeed But instead of just individual technology skills, we're looking for a new colleague with a caring and easy-going mindset, enthusiasm, and eagerness to help and learn new things .

What we offer Modern working culture that supports your personal goals and growth A chance to have a real impact on how data platforms are built and scaled Work with leading companies across the EMEA region Hybrid or remote setup - we work fluently in both environments Opportunities to expand your know-how across data engineering, cloud, and AI Competitive salary and a wide range of perks and benefits A genuinely supportive, low-hierarchy team that values collaboration and curiosity What to expect We aim to make the hiring process as smooth as our product.

We believe we ensure the best fit for you and ADE with a prescreening call, two rounds of interviews, and reference checks before the decision.

Ready to make data architecture smarter? If this sounds like your next challenge, we'd love to hear from you.
