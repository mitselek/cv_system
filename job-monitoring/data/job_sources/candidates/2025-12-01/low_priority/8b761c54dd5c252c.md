## Job Description

Aalto University is where science and art meet technology and business.

We shape a sustainable future by making research breakthroughs in and across our disciplines, sparking the game changers of tomorrow and creating novel solutions to major global challenges.

Our community is made up of 120 nationalities, 14 000 students, 400 professors and close to 5000 faculty and staff working on our dynamic campus in Espoo, Greater Helsinki, Finland.

Diversity is part of who we are, and we actively work to ensure our community's diversity and inclusiveness.

This is why we warmly encourage qualified candidates from all backgrounds to join our community.

Aalto Finance Services supports the university in its core actions by ensuring financial sustainability in the long term, providing professional support for academic units and ensuring compliance.

The Process & System Support team develops and supports all of Aalto's Financial Systems and their users.

We are looking for a Finance System Solution Analyst to design, develop, and support Aalto University's financial processes and systems.

Your role and goals As a Finance System Solution Analyst, you will help ensure that our finance systems meet the needs of the organization while maintaining stability and efficiency.

You will collaborate closely with Finance users, Finance Management, Aalto Schools, Support Functions, and external system and service providers.

Your work will cover systems and processes related to Accounting, Planning, Payments, Banking, and other Finance operations.

You will report to the Manager of the Finance Process and Systems Support team.

Main responsibilities System support and maintenance Handle and resolve support tickets (incidents, problems, questions) from Aalto users Manage and follow up on support requests with system vendors Take initiative to improve system usability and user satisfaction Manage and regularly review system access rights Perform system administration tasks for time-critical Finance operations (e.g.

month-end and year-end closing) Carry out system maintenance tasks such as housekeeping, regular updates, and data integrity checks Keep system configuration and documentation up to date Development and improvement Identify and prepare system development needs for approval Manage and follow up on approved developments and testing Participate in user training and maintain training materials Represent the Finance organization in university-wide development projects when needed Vendor management Participate in regular operational meetings with vendors Prepare and present summaries of vendor performance (e.g.

ticket statistics, service levels) What we expect from you You are proactive, development-oriented, and comfortable working both independently and with diverse stakeholders.

You communicate clearly and approach problem-solving analytically.

We expect experience with modern ERP (cloud) systems, in particular Oracle Fusion Cloud experience with Projects (PPM) and Finance (GA) modules several years of experience with Financial systems and/or ERP Finance development projects either in consulting, corporate development or public sector working background in operational Finance / Financial Management functions or equivalent experience strong analytical and problem-solving skills working proficiency in both Finnish and English higher education degree in e.g.

Finance, Business Administration, or related field Ambition to grow and willingness to take on additional responsibilities, for example (substitute) managerial tasks and larger developments What we offer With the deployment of the new Oracle Fusion Cloud ERP financial system, there is a significant need to support the adaptation of the new system and to improve processes across the whole university.

You'll have a unique opportunity to contribute and make your mark You will be working together with our small team in common financial services as a part of the Process & System support team.

Our team has a good mix of Financial and System knowledge to find the right solutions based on needs and system capabilities.
