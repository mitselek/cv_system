## Job Description

We're looking for a Product Manager, B2B to lead the development of Singa's business-to-business products and shape the future of how venues and hospitality partners deliver karaoke experiences worldwide.

You'll take full ownership of the B2B product area, driving strategy, discovery, prioritization, and delivery from start to finish.

The goal is to build products that delight users and become indispensable to our customers' businesses, while maintaining a healthy balance between speed, quality, and long-term scalability.

This is a key role in Singa's product organization, as the team evolves into dedicated sub-teams focused on different business areas.

You'll lead the B2B product stream - ensuring its roadmap delivers measurable business impact, customer satisfaction, and growth.

You'll collaborate closely with engineering, design, sales, customer success, and data to align priorities, streamline delivery, and continuously improve how we plan, build, and measure our products.

What Success Looks Like The B2B roadmap is clear, data-driven, and tied to customer and business outcomes.

Product delivery runs on schedule with strong alignment and focus across teams.

Features launch successfully, adoption grows, and customer satisfaction increases.

The product team operates efficiently, with fewer ad-hoc fixes and higher release quality.

You'll be expected to challenge assumptions, connect commercial goals with technical reality, and continuously improve how we plan, build, and measure our products.

You stay calm under pressure and make clear, informed decisions when priorities compete.

What You'll Be Doing Owning the B2B product roadmap - defining, prioritising, and communicating upcoming initiatives based on customer and business needs Driving product delivery - ensuring features ship on schedule and with high quality, documented accordingly Improving Product KPIs (cycle time, release frequency, crash-free rates) and both customer and user satisfaction Ensuring cross-team alignment - making priorities, trade-offs, and goals visible and understood Leading discovery and validation - gathering insights from users, and data to get insight on what to build next Monitoring outcomes - measure post-release impact and adjust roadmap accordingly Challenging roadmaps and technical decisions -
