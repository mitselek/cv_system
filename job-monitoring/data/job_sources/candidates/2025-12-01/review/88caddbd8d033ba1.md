JOIN YOUSICIAN Yousician impacts and enriches the lives of millions of people by empowering them to play music they love, learn something new, and nourish their well-being.

If you are looking for a rewarding career opportunity that helps shape the future of music-tech, you're in the right place! WHY YOU SHOULD APPLY Yousician develops cutting-edge audio technology that gives users real-time feedback on how they play.

As Developer for Audio and Machine Learning, you'll be an important part in integrating the technology into the product, by developing solid cross-platform utilities for audio processing and deep learning.

You'll also take part in designing the software architecture.

In this role, you'll have a unique opportunity to profoundly impact the lives of hundreds of millions of future musicians worldwide and re-imagine the way music education works.

WHAT YOU'LL DO Develop our audio processing and DSP utilities.

Work on both native (modern C++ with platform specific parts in Java and Objective-C) and managed code (C#).

Implement platform integrations where required.

Be in close connection with our content creation to connect audio tech to the content Work in a small team, taking ownership of major parts of the product.

WHO YOU ARE You have proven, strong development skills.

You have some knowledge about audio signal processing and realtime programming You have experience with automated testing.

Familiarity with Unity, Jenkins or basics of music theory is an asset.

Experience in native development (Android/iOS/macOS/Windows) development is an asset.

You aren't afraid of getting your hands dirty with low level integrations and debugging.

You are able to get up to speed quickly with existing code.

You learn fast, work independently and get things done.

WHAT WE OFFER You'll have an opportunity to join our international team, backed by the top VC's and competing with the best education applications in the world You'll have a meaningful and profound impact on the lives of millions of musicians and future musicians all around the world.

You'll get to work with a passionate and friendly growing band of PMs, designers, developers and data scientists.

You'll get a competitive compensation package - salary, perks- and work in an awesome office in the heart of Helsinki.
