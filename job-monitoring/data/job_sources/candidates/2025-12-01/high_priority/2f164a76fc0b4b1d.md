## Job Description

We are looking for a Solution Analyst to join OP Pohjola in Helsinki.

The project/team, as a part of a larger architectural transformation, is acting as a spearhead in utilizing modern data and technology capabilities to develop a new credit risk model implementation framework and data products which are used for internal credit risk measurement, calculation, reporting and control in OP Pohjola.

The existing, complex business environment and the development of data architecture pose significant requirements for understanding the holistic data landscape as well as designing and implementing future proof, scalable data processes.

Your role in a nutshell In your role as a Solution Analyst, you play a key role in bridging business needs with technical solutions.

You analyze and formalize implementation requirements, understand the workload and priorities, identify improvement opportunities and design scalable solutions.

You collaborate closely with stakeholders, developers and product owners to ensure successful and timely implementation.

Key responsibilities: Requirements Gathering & Analysis: Discussing with stakeholders to understand business needs and translate them into functional and technical requirements.

Documentation & Communication: Create clear documentation including use cases, process flows, and technical specifications.

Communicate effectively across teams.

Solution Design: Develop solution concepts and workflows, ensuring alignment with enterprise architecture and business objectives.

Testing & Validation: Support QA activities by defining test cases, validating outcomes, and ensuring solutions meet acceptance criteria.

Does this sound like you? Your key qualifications: Master's degree or higher in a relevant field Proven experience in business analysis or solution design within complex environments Strong understanding of software development principles and data modeling Strong capabilities in both SQL and Python Familiarity with cloud environments and knowledge of azure Proficiency in Jira and version control systems like Git Excellent problem-solving, communication, and stakeholder management skills Familiarity with Agile methodologies and CI/CD practices Understanding of banking and risk management Your soft skills: Active listening and questioning Creative and critical thinking Ability to give and receive constructive feedback Presentation skills A few words about us Our department is responsible for data related processes and operations of OP Pohjola Risk Management.

You would be joining our analytics implementation team that currently has about 15 amazing professionals.

The team develops data products, APIs and implements credit risk models.
