## Job Description

Meet Sievo We are Sievo - a fast-growing Software-as-a-Service company from Finland.

Our unique superpower is called procurement analytics.

We are the global leaders in our space and help the largest companies in the world to understand their procurement data, make data-driven decisions, reduce emissions, drive supply chain resilience, and beyond! Each year, we process over 2% of the world's total spend.

Pretty cool, right? At Sievo, we're powered by smart and kind people from all over the world.

Our 400+ Sievonians are based in Helsinki, Chicago and Bucharest.

The role Want to take us to the next level? We are now looking for a Senior Product Manager to lead the Procurement Performance Management product team in Helsinki, a highly capable team building one of the key product areas of Sievo.

This role ensures the product stays on the right course, the team focuses on what matters most, and the business continues to grow.

As one of our flagship products, its ongoing success makes this position critical to our future.

What will you be doing as a Senior Product Manager? Interact with customers, prospects, and external stakeholders to gain a deep understanding of their needs to define the right problems to solve Curiously learn about new trends and competition in the market Use all the above information and your own expertise to build a product vision and strategy and drive this product group to success Prioritize development and plan and deliver your roadmap with the development team; own your domain with the team from research, discovery, design, development to maintenance and communication Lead one of Sievo's product development teams, utilizing your leadership and coaching skills (while not necessarily being the manager of (all) the team members) Ensure that your development team has the customer and business context needed Collaborate with sales and marketing to create go-to-market plans and strategies to build the business Work in a quickly evolving field, where understanding the big picture from the noise is needed Report to Sievo's VP of Product Management To succeed in this role, you have Relevant experience in product management, ideally in B2B SaaS A proven ability to lead teams and work cross-functionally in a highly collaborative environment Excellent communication skills, and ability to turn complex problems into understandable messages that all parties from engineers to CEO understand.

In other words, you can manage communications in a complicated domain with many stakeholders Result-driven and ambitious mindset, willing to go the extra mile when needed A passion for exploring customer problems and transforming them into valuable solutions.

You are genuinely interested in understanding your customers and their needs Fluency in English You can dream big, build a vision for your product and build a bridge between now and the future You don't need to be technical in this role, but interest and understanding of technology, data, and analytics and utilizing them to help our clients achieve what they need with our products is a must Seen as a plus Consulting experience Supply chain / procurement understanding We offer you You'll get to build the leading procurement analytics software that helps a rapidly growing number of Fortune 500 companies across the globe to understand and optimize their spend and supply chain The chance to have a strong impact on Sievo's product and future A chance to work with other highly ambitious, driven and smart people Opportunity to develop and expand your skills in Product Management and B2B Enterprise SaaS business Freedom and responsibility.

We are looking for people who yearn for this freedom, can make the most out of it and are willing to take on responsibility One of the coolest offices in the heart of Helsinki - or if you prefer, a flexible hybrid working model.

We also provide all essential tools to work remotely International team members coming from over 40 nationalities Awesome perks including equity program, extensive healthcare, phone plan, home internet connection, referral bonus, and many more! This is a full-time position, and we will fill the position as soon as we have found a suitable candidate.
