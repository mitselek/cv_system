## STAR Method Refresher

Use this structure for behavioral questions:

**Situation**: Set the context (where, when, what project)
**Task**: Explain the challenge or responsibility
**Action**: Describe what YOU did (focus on your contribution)
**Result**: Share the outcome and what you learned

### Example for RmIT

**Q: "Tell me about a challenging technical problem you solved."**

**S**: "At P√ñFF, we had performance issues with the festival platform during peak ticket sales periods."

**T**: "I needed to identify bottlenecks and optimize the system without disrupting the ongoing festival operations."

**A**: "I profiled the database queries, identified N+1 query problems, implemented query optimization, added caching layers, and restructured the API response format."

**R**: "We reduced page load times by 60% and handled peak traffic without issues during the festival. More importantly, I documented the optimization patterns for the team to apply elsewhere."
